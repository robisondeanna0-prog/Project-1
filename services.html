Service and Maintenance

At Cartier, service is an art. Advice, maintenance, repairs, adjustment or personalization: the Maison is committed to creating a unique, rich and vibrant relationship of trust with each of its customers, to building on it over time. To make every moment an encounter between you and Cartier.

Enhancing the Gem's Sparkle

For Cartier, stones are eternal and their beauty transcends time. For their original intensity to last, they need to be treated with the same care as everything else we love. A few precautions and maintenance tips will keep them always shining and free from imperfections. If you have any questions, we invite you to contact our advisors, who will be able to answer them.

Set for you by Cartier

Set for you by Cartier is a range of services dedicated to personalizing your Cartier creations, from jewelry to watches and accessories. The Maison offers to engrave initials or a handwritten message on many of its creations, and to personalize the box with initials or a symbolic date hot-stamped on it.

Maintenance and Repairs

From master jewelers to polishers, our craftspeople are committed to creating pieces designed to last through the ages. The Maison offers you a range of maintenance, adjustment and repair services which will revitalize the shine and adjust pieces if technically achievable.
